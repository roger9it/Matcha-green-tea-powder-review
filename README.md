## What Is Matcha Green Tea Powder?

**Matcha green tea powder** is a finely ground powder made from specially grown green tea leaves. Unlike regular green tea, where you steep the leaves and discard them, matcha allows you to consume the entire leaf — giving you more nutrients and antioxidants.

It originated in Japan and has been used for centuries for its health benefits and calming energy boost.
